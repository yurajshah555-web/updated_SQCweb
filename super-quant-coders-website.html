About Super Quant Coders

Who We Are

Super Quant Coders is the flagship technical club of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology, Jalandhar. Established in 2018, we are a community of passionate coders, quantum enthusiasts, robotics experts, and tech innovators dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of innovation, competitive programming, and technological excellence among students. We organize workshops, hackathons, robotics competitions, and quantum computing seminars to equip students with industry-relevant skills.

Our Vision

To be recognized as one of India's leading technical clubs, producing world-class developers, researchers, and innovators who contribute to the global tech ecosystem.
